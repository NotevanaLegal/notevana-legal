Frequently Asked Questions

  • What instrument is Fretboard Practice for? Fretboard Practice is designed for standard 6-string guitar tuning (E A D G B E). It is not intended for bass.
  • Why does the app ask for microphone access? Microphone access is used for pitch detection in Fretboard Practice and Tuner.
  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Open the App Store, tap your account, then go to Subscriptions to manage Notevana Pro.
  • Do I need an account to use the app? No. Notevana does not require account sign-in.

When Reporting a Problem

Please include the following so we can help faster:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • Your Notevana app version
  • A short description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible
