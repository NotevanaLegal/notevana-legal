Notevana Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2025

Notevana respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how that information is used, and how subscription-related information is handled.

Information We Access

Notevana may request access to your device microphone so the app can detect musical pitch during fretboard and tuning features.

Notevana also stores limited app preferences on your device, such as:

  • onboarding completion
  • selected strings
  • selected note pool
  • pitch tolerance and practice settings
  • sound and playback preferences

How Information Is Used

Microphone access is used only to analyze pitch in real time while you use music practice features inside the app.

App preferences are stored locally so your settings and progress-related selections can persist between launches.

Audio Data

Notevana is not designed to record, store, or upload your microphone audio for its core functionality. Microphone input is used for live pitch detection on your device.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription through Apple In-App Purchase, Apple handles payment processing, billing, renewals, and subscription management. Notevana does not receive your full payment information.

Data Sharing

Notevana does not sell your personal information.

Notevana does not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary for Apple to provide In-App Purchase, billing, and subscription services.

Third-Party Services

Notevana relies on Apple services for App Store distribution, In-App Purchase processing, and subscription management. Those services are governed by Apple's own terms, policies, and privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Notevana is not intended to collect personal information from children knowingly.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Notevana after an update means the revised policy will apply.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact notevana74@gmail.com or visit the Notevana support page.
